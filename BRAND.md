# Solven Advisory — Brand Reference

This is a flattened markdown reference for the Solven Advisory brand, optimised
for agent consumption during development. The full brand guidelines live in
`Solven_Brand_Guidelines.docx` (human-readable) — this file is the
machine-readable equivalent.

## The brand in one sentence

Solven Advisory helps private equity firms in Portugal adopt AI without losing
the analytical judgment that makes their work defensible.

## Core thesis

Augmentation without cognitive atrophy. The risk in enterprise AI rollouts is
not adoption failure but cognitive offloading — analysts becoming dependent on
drafts they cannot fully defend. Solven sells judgment, not tools.

## Logo

The wordmark is "Solven" in Georgia Regular, with the lowercase v replaced by
a sage-green chevron — a solid triangle with a smaller triangular cutout.
The chevron is a fixed vector path, never a typeset character.

### Clear space

Minimum clear space around any lockup = the cap height of the wordmark
(distance from baseline to top of capital S). No element may intrude.

### Dark mode

Background: `#1F2420` (deeper than the wordmark charcoal).
On dark: wordmark switches to Bone, chevron switches to Sage (lighter).

### Rules

- The chevron is the only sage element on any given lockup.
- Never use any green other than the two approved sages.
- Never use pure black for the wordmark — Charcoal only.
- Never introduce additional brand colours. The palette is intentionally narrow.

## Typography

System fonts only. Georgia (serif), Calibri (sans). Both available on every
Windows, Mac, iOS, Android device. No webfonts, no @font-face.

### Hierarchy

| Role             | Face    | Size   | Colour     | Notes                              |
| ---------------- | ------- | ------ | ---------- | ---------------------------------- |
| H1               | Georgia | 28pt   | Charcoal   | Regular weight only                |
| H2               | Georgia | 18pt   | Charcoal   | Regular weight only                |
| H3 / Section     | Calibri | 11pt   | Sage Deep  | Bold, ALL CAPS, 20% tracking       |
| Lead paragraph   | Georgia | 14pt   | Grey Mid   | Italic                             |
| Body             | Calibri | 11pt+  | Charcoal   | Web minimum 17px                   |
| Caption          | Calibri | 9pt    | Grey Light | Italic                             |
| ADVISORY         | Calibri | varies | Sage Deep  | ALL CAPS, 32% tracking             |

### Rules

- Never use Georgia Bold. Use larger sizes for emphasis.
- Never use ALL CAPS in Georgia. All caps is reserved for Calibri designators.
- Never justify body text. Always left-aligned, ragged right.
- Never introduce a third typeface.

## Voice

- Considered, not corporate. Direct, not blunt.
- Complete sentences. No fragments for emphasis.
- Specific over generic.
- Avoid: synergy, leverage (verb), value-add, robust, holistic, leading,
  cutting-edge, unlock, ecosystem, journey, delight.
- When in doubt, fewer words.
