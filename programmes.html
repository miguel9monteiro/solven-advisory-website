The thread

Every session carries the analyst-first principle.

Analysts produce the formative work first, then bring Claude in as a critic, a red-teamer, and a verifier.

The risk in any rollout is that talented people become editors of drafts they cannot fully defend. We design every session the other way round. Verification habits, the devil's advocate pattern, AI as critic rather than author — the same skills that unconstrained use erodes are the ones we build.

That is what we mean by augmentation without cognitive atrophy, and it runs through all four sessions and everything that follows them.
