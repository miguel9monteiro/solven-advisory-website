Capability 01

Diligence · financing · investment committee

Firm-wide enablement, plus agents on the heaviest reviews.

Enablement + build

Context

Diligence, financing, and investment-committee workflows are repetitive and judgment-heavy — exactly the work where a team wants genuine fluency rather than a one-off demonstration.

Approach

A monthly enablement programme across the whole firm, analyst to partner, running alongside a build track that turns the most repetitive, judgment-heavy reviews into supervised agents.

What we build

  • Contract redlining agent Agent

    Extraction and comparison of lease and financing terms across documents, surfacing deviations for a human reviewer to rule on.

  • IC memo interrogation agent Agent

    Adversarial stress-testing of investment-committee papers before presentation: the devil's advocate pattern, operationalised.

Capability 02

Portfolio operations

Portfolio reporting, aggregated where founders already are.

Build

Context

Funds with large portfolios collect reporting through whichever channel each founder prefers. Quarterly aggregation is manual, slow, and brittle.

Email WhatsApp Notion Excel PowerPoint

Approach

An AI ingestion, normalization, and verification layer that meets founders in their existing channels, then reconciles everything into one consistent portfolio view, with the source for every figure preserved.

The point

No founder is asked to change how they report. The fund gets a single, verifiable picture of the portfolio, and the team's time moves from assembling the data to interrogating it.

Capability 03

Discovery & scoping

Scoping across deal evaluation and memo workflows.

Scoping

Context

Before any build, mapping where AI belongs across deal evaluation and investment-memo workflows — and, just as important, where it does not.

Approach

A scoping engagement: mapping the workflows worth automating, the judgment that must stay human, and the enablement the team needs to operate either way with confidence.
