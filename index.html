AI Diligence

Find out whether the AI is real.

When a target or a founder claims to be “AI-powered,” we give the fund a defensible read on what is genuine capability and what is marketing jargon, before the term sheet.

The pitch

  • “Proprietary AI engine.”
  • “AI-native from day one.”
  • “Our models are the moat.”
  • “The workflow is fully automated.”

What we surface

  • The stack behind the story: built in-house, or a thin wrapper on a model anyone can rent.
  • Whether the data and models are genuinely defensible, or commodity dressed up as a moat.
  • Whether the team can ship what they claim, at the velocity they claim.
  • Where AI actually touches the P&L, and where it is theatre for the raise.

The deliverable: a clear read for the investment committee — what is real, what is veneer, and what it changes about the price.
